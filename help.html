Help

1. Getting Started

Welcome to Jungle! To get started, simply create an account, browse through our products, and begin shopping. If you need help at any step, you can always refer to this page or contact support.

2. Creating an Account

To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button located in the top right corner of the homepage. Provide your name, email address, and create a password to set up your account.

3. Placing an Order

Once you're logged in, browse through the products and add them to your cart. When you're ready to checkout, go to your cart and complete your purchase by providing shipping and payment details.

4. Payment Options

We accept various payment methods, including credit/debit cards, UPI, and net banking. Choose your preferred method during checkout and follow the prompts to make the payment.

5. Order Tracking

After placing an order, you will receive a tracking number. You can track your order by logging into your account and checking the "Order History" section.

6. Returns and Refunds

If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you can return the product within 30 days. Please visit our Returns and Refunds Policy for more details.

7. Contact Support

If you need further assistance or have any questions, feel free to contact our support team at support@jungle.com.

8. Frequently Asked Questions

For quick answers to common queries, check out our FAQ page.

9. Troubleshooting

If you're experiencing issues with our website or services, try clearing your browser cache or using a different browser. You can also contact our support team for further troubleshooting.
